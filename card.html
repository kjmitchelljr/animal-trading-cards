Gray Wolf

Social creatures, gray wolves have a complex communication system that involves body language, barking, growling, “dancing,” howling and scent making.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Length: 3.4 – 5.2 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 6 to 8 years
  • Habitat: Tundra, Mountain areas, Eoodlands, Forests, Grasslands and Deserts

Wolves are the largest members of the dog family. Adaptable gray wolves are by far the most common and were once found all over the Northern Hemisphere.
